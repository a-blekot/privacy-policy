### **Data Deleted and Retained**

Upon receiving a verified deletion request, **Lucky Time** will **permanently delete** the following data:

* **Personal Identifiable Information (PII):** Your name, date of birth, and any other personal details you provided during account creation or profile setup.
* **Account Identifiers:** The unique identifiers linked to your Google or Apple Sign-in, as well as any internal user ID we have assigned.
* **App Activity and Usage Data:** All usage statistics, session history, and in-app activity tracking.

#### **Data Retention**

We are committed to deleting all user data upon request, but we may retain certain non-personal, anonymized data, or data necessary for legal and regulatory purposes:

* **Payment Records:** Transactional data related to in-app purchases or subscriptions will be retained for up to 7 years to comply with accounting, tax, and regulatory compliance laws. This data is not linked to your deleted account.
* **Fraud Prevention & Security Logs:** Basic server access logs, which may include anonymized device information and IP addresses, are retained for up to 90 days for security and fraud prevention purposes.
